PyMGC3

PyMGC3 is a Python toolkit to apply the Modified Great Circle Cell Counts (mGC3) method, originally devised by Mateu et al. (2011) to search for tidal streams in the Galactic Halo.


The current distribution computes pole count maps using the full mGC3/nGC3/GC3 family of methods described in Mateu et al. 2011 (MNRAS, 415, 214) and Abedi et al. 2014 (MNRAS, 442, 3627).
